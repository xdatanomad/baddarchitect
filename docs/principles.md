# Principles

## Editorial

- Be direct, candid, practical, and humble.
- Avoid hype, inflated claims, and broad unsupported AI predictions.
- Separate opinion, experience, and cited external claims.
- Give readers a usable takeaway: a checklist, decision rule, example, blueprint, or exercise.

## Website

- Keep the site static-first, fast, readable, accessible, and easy to maintain.
- Prefer calm editorial design over promotional or generic AI visuals.
- Keep live website content in `website/src/content/`.
- Keep raw notes, drafts, and source material outside the deployable site until intentionally published.

## Repository

- Preserve source material when reorganizing.
- Use `docs/` for durable project direction and decisions.
- Use `content/` for editorial workflow.
- Use `research/` for evidence, source tracking, and learning notes.
- Use `.agents/` for local agent skills, checklists, workflows, and project configuration.

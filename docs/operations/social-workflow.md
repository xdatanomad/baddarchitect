# Social Workflow

## Folders

- `content/social/linkedin-plans/` - article-specific LinkedIn promotion plans.
- `content/social/linkedin-short-posts/` - short LinkedIn posts and short video scripts.
- `content/social/linkedin-long-posts/` - longer LinkedIn article summaries or essays.

## Rules

- Tie each social asset to a source article or lesson.
- Preserve the direct, candid, practical project tone.
- Do not turn nuanced articles into hype-heavy claims.
- Prefer practical hooks, specific examples, and clear reader value.

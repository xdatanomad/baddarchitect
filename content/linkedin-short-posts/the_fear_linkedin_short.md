## Post Description

The first bottleneck in AI adoption is not technology.
It is fear.

When employees hesitate to use AI, leaders often label it as resistance to change.

But beneath that hesitation may be real concerns:

- Will this replace my job?
- Will this be used to cut people instead of support them?
- Where should AI not act on its own?
- What safeguards actually exist?

Responsible AI adoption does not start by dismissing fear.

It starts by listening closely enough to turn vague anxiety into explicit decisions about workflow, governance, human review, and trust.

Before pushing another AI rollout, ask people what they are afraid AI will change.

That answer is useful design input.
